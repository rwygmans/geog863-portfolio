Welcome to Trail Issue Reporter

Help keep trails safe and enjoyable by reporting maintenance issues.

How it works

  1. Open the form: Tap the Report Issue button at the bottom.
  2. Set location: Use Current location or Tap map to drop a pin.
  3. Describe the issue: Choose a type, add a short description, and pick a severity.
  4. Add a photo (optional): A picture helps staff assess and prioritize.
  5. Submit: Your report appears on the map and is shared with maintainers.
